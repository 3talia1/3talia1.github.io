What is Self Care? üíå

Self care is defined as prioritizing yourself and your mental health. Self-care means taking care of yourself so that you can be healthy, you can be well, you can do your job, you can help and care for others, and you can do all the things you need to and want to accomplish in a day.

How is Self Care benficial? ‚òªÔ∏é

Self care has all types of benefit that i'll get into later. A few common benefits include staying mentally, physically, and emotionally well. Self-care is anything you do to take care of yourself so you can stay physically, mentally, and emotionally well. Its benefits are better physical, mental, and emotional health and well-being. There are different types of self care.

Types of Self Care

Emotional Self-Care

Emotional self care is actions such as self-talk, weekly bubble baths, saying ‚Äúno‚Äù to things that cause unnecessary stress, giving yourself permission to take a pause, or setting up a weekly coffee date with a friend

Physical Self-Care

Physical self care is prioritizing sleep, adopting an exercise routine you can stick with, or choosing healthy and nourishing foods over highly processed ones.

Spiritual Self-Care

Spiritual self care is attending a religious service, spending time in nature, meditating, incorporating regular acts of kindness into your day, or keeping a gratitude journal.
